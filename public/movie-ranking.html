My top 3 movies of all-time

2012

Directed by Roland Emmerich, "2012" is a disaster film depicting a global cataclysm occurring in the year 2012 as predicted by the ancient Mayans. It follows several characters' attempts to survive as various natural disasters threaten to destroy the world.

The Vault

"The Vault" is a Spanish heist thriller film. The plot revolves around a group of skilled criminals who plan to rob a bank's vault filled with valuable treasure. However, they soon discover that there's something much more sinister hidden within the vault's depths.

Wall Street

"Wall Street" is a drama film set in the high-stakes world of finance. It follows a young and ambitious stockbroker, Bud Fox, as he becomes involved with the unscrupulous corporate raider Gordon Gekko. The film explores themes of greed, ambition, and morality in the world of finance.
